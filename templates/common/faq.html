General Questions

To register on DBIT Alumni Hub:

  1. Click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar
  2. Select your role (Student, Alumni, or Faculty)
  3. Fill in your details including name, email, phone, and role-specific information
  4. Submit the form and check your email for verification (if applicable)
  5. Log in with your credentials

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password
If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Networking & Connections

To connect with alumni:

  1. Browse the Alumni Directory from your dashboard
  2. Use filters to find alumni by department, graduation year, or company
  3. Click on a profile to view details
  4. Click the "Connect" button to send a connection request
  5. Wait for the alumni to accept your request

Tip: Personalize your profile to increase the chances of connection acceptance!

You can manage connection requests from your dashboard:

  • View Requests: Check the "Pending Requests" section on your dashboard
  • Accept: Click the green checkmark to accept a request
  • Reject: Click the red X to decline a request

You'll receive notifications when someone sends you a connection request.

Profile Management

To update your profile:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your name in the navigation bar
  3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown
  4. Click the "Edit Profile" button
  5. Update your information and click "Save Changes"

You can update your photo, bio, professional details, and social media links.

Supported image formats:

  • PNG (.png)
  • JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
  • GIF (.gif)
Maximum file size: 16MB

Technical Support

We appreciate bug reports! To report a bug:

  1. Go to the Report Issue page
  2. Select "Bug Report" as the issue type
  3. Provide detailed information about the bug
  4. Include steps to reproduce the issue
  5. Submit the form

Our team will investigate and get back to you as soon as possible.
